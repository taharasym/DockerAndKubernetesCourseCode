Kubernetes what does it do

Kubernetes is the conductor

Docker does the starting, stopping and deleting containers, low level stuff

Kubernetes cares about higher-level stuff like

- Scheduling, scaling, healing, updating

- How may containers to run in, maybe which node to run them on

- Things like knowing when to scale them up or down

- How to update your container without downtime

we would have a  Kubernetes cluster  to host our applications
