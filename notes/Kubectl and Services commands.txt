Kubectl and Services commands

k create -f file.service.yml --save-config
k apply -f file.service.yml
k delete -f file.service.yml

Testing a Service and Pod with curl

k exec [pod-name] -- curl -s http://podIP

Not every image will have a curl command so you can shell into the container

k exec [pod-name] -it -- sh
apk add curl
curl -s http://podIP


Service are like a gateway into pods


k apply -f clusterIP.service.yml
