Deployments and ReplicaSet

A replica set is declarative way to manage Pods

Deployments and ReplicaSets(scale the pods) ensure Pods stay running and can be used to scale Pods

A Deployment manages pods
 
- Pods ae managed using ReplicaSets
- Scales ReplicaSets, which scale Pods
- Support zero-downtime updates by creating and destroying ReplicaSets
- Self-healing mechanism
- Provide fault-tolerance
- Provide rollback Functionality
- Create a unique label that is assigned to the ReplicaSet and generate pods
